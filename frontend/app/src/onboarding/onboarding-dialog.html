Alius is latin for another or diverse. Nowadays, it's very easy to get captured by a single side of any story.

Alius provides an alternative news search app where every side of a story is represented to widen your horizons.

Alius utilizes IBM Watson to evaluate the tone of news articles and scores them in 5 emotions. The shade of the color relates to the strength of the perceived tone.
Joy
Fear
Anger
Disgust
Sadness

Alius lets you search for news, see their predominant sentiments, save the articles you like, and explore your preferred moods.

Send us feedback! We are happy to offer you a wider view of the world.
